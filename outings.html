Outings

Outings is an iOS application for tracking when and where users have gone in a certain time frame. The idea came about when our team of three students was casually discussing what we had done the previous weekend and realized we couldn't really remember the exact details of what we had done. The app allows users to retrace their steps on a trip with a "cookie trail" style interactive map. It incorporates a social aspect by allowing friends to share their paths and plan new locations to meet up. In reality, the app was made to remember the exact trail from a night out at bars, but this technology can be applied to other activities from hiking to vacations, or simply any time people want to map their steps of the places they visited.

The app is built on a MVC architecture with the main navigation handled by a UITabBarController. For the backend, Google's new Firestore document style database was used. This comes with the same Firebase authentication to streamline login/signup through email or Facebook accounts. Details of the different features and open sources libraries are discussed below. To navigate to the details about specific sections/pages of the app, use the menu bar on the right.

Authentication

To login or signup, AMLoginSignup was used as a template to create a sleek and simple introduction to the users. The page animates between the login and signup sections or the user can simply click the Facebook button to be authenticated through the Facebook SDK. Users and their information are stored in documents in the "Users" collection which is discussed in more detail in the database section.
