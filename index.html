Hi, I'm Ryan

I recently graduated from San José State University with a degree in Computer Engineering. As fun as it was learning all the low-level architecture and embedded systems, I've found my true passion to be in iOS development. I love following all things Swift from weekly newsletters to podcasts. I'm eager to start my journey with an exciting and challenging position in mobile development.

Check out some of my favorite projects I completed both at school and on the side along the way. The about me goes into detail of some other fun ways I like to spend my time. Contact info can be found at the bottom of this page.
