About

I currently live in the SF Bay Area and am finishing up my B.S. in Computer Engineering at San Jose State University. I enjoy working on my electric longboard and developing iPhone applications.

Projects

Electric Skateboard: "The Bronco"

I have always been fascinated with electric vehicles so I decided to build an electric skateboard to improve my commute to school. I started researching for the project in the summer of 2015 and the build begin in early 2016. Although the board is fully operational, I am still adding improvements. The current enhancements include adding a BMS for easier charging/cell balancing and incorporating an iOS app to monitor the speed and various settings for the board. The link above outlines the project from its inception to the current improvements being implemented.
For build specifications and updates click here.

iOS Application: "SpartaMart"

SpartaMart is an iPhone application that allows SJSU students to buy goods from one another in a safe and comfortable manner. The project began as an assignment to understand the software lifecycle of an application in the fall of 2016. I have continued to work on the application and hope to have it submitted to the app store by April of 2017.

FSAE Electric: "SRE Test Board"

In 2015-2016 I had a small role on the Spartan Racing Electric team where I built a test board to verify the BMS and current sensor were functioning properly for inspection. The board is Arduino based and uses a 16x2 LCD to display the sensor data.

More Stuff will be added here. For now check out my gitHub to find ongoing prijects.

The Bronco

Research for this build started in early 2015 and numerous upgrades are still in progress that will be updated to this page. This write up follows the project in chronological order so for the current working board specs and in progress developments, navigate to the bottom of this page.

Parts List

Power System:

Battery: 10S1P 5aH (Turnigy 5Ah 2S 60C Hardcase Packs x5)
Battery Management System: 42V-10S-60A LiPo BMS
Charger: 42V-2A Fast Charger
Charger Replacement Plug: 3 Pin Inline
Charge Port: 3-Pin Inline Port
Switch: Latching LED Pushbutton SPDT
Voltmeter: 5-120V DC LED meter

Drive System:

Motor Controller: VESC
Motor: Turnigy SK3-6374-192kv Brushless DC
Belt: 15mm HTD5-55T (275mm)
Gearing: 36T/16T 2.25 Ratio
Motor Mount: TorqueBoards v2
Transmitter: 2.4gHz Mini Remote

Board & Accessories

Deck: 40” LHB Scarlet Electric Skateboard Deck
Wheels: 83mm ABEC 11 75a Flywheels
Enclosure: Custom Psycotiller 18” ABS Plastic
Enclosure Mount: M6 Wood Insert Screws
Trucks: Caliber II 50-degree
Lights: 3V-LED array
Risers: 0.5” Khiro Flat
Hardware: 1.5”
Bearings: Bones Reds (with spacers)
Miscellaneous Parts: 10AWG wire/5mm bullet connectors/Heat shrink tubing

Early Prototyping

I began getting the parts together for this build in late 2015. As I was still deciding on a deck to use, a basic skateboard deck was used for testing.
